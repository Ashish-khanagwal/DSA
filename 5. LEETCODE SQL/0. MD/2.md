Not every person in the database may have an address.  
To ensure we include **all people**, even those without address entries,

## ğŸ§© Approach

1. Select columns from both tables â€” `firstName`, `lastName`, `city`, and `state`.
2. Join the two tables using **LEFT JOIN** on the common column `personId`.

3. A `LEFT JOIN` ensures that:
   - Every person appears in the result.
   - If a person has no matching record in the `Address` table,
